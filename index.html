ABOUT COMPANY

We heard you when you said, “Wish cooking was done by a click of a button!” We are a bunch of food enthusiasts who wish to revolutionize the way a home kitchen feels, looks and cooks. Our products are IoT-based smart machines that not only make the whole cooking process simple but also clean it selves after. Just by a click of a button. You can now say goodbye to the excuses to eat unhealthy outside food and say hello to perfectly home-cooked, delicious meals.

OUR TEAM

Generation Infinite is founded by a seasoned multi-disciplinary team with expertise in product design, embedded programming and business, passionate in getting to the roots of futuristic innovation. We have degrees in engineering, technology and design and a common love for homemade, Mom’s food. Having a right team by side always ensures the road to be adventurous; we are just that!

OUR FLAGSHIP PRODUCT

SmartBatter is a smart machine that can store ingredients and make your favourite batter ready, when you want it to be. You don’t have to worry cleaning up later. It takes care of itself. Also, your batter is already fermented and refrigerated at the optimal temperatures. Again, all this while you are still at work, shopping at a mall, or enjoying a fan moment in a movie hall!
